Singing Voice Synthesis (SVS)

Introduction:

  • Score-based singing voice synthesis refers to synthesizing the singing voice from the given lyrics and the musical scores.
  • We provide several samples for each singing type. Each item contains input conditions (lyrics and musical score), and audio synthesized by three different models (FFT-Singer, GAN-Singer and DiffSinger).
  • In addition, we also mix the singing voice synthesized by DiffSinger and the musical score to get Mixed Sound, which is used to demonstrate the harmony between the synthesized audio and the score.
